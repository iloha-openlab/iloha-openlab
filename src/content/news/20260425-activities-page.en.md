# Representative's Activities page is now live

We have updated the ILOHA OpenLab LLC website and published a new "[Activities](/en/activities/)" page for our Representative, Hiroshi Miura.

This page summarizes the Representative's long-term contributions to open-source software (OSS) development, maintenance, and involvement in open data communities such as OpenStreetMap.

ILOHA OpenLab was founded on the foundation of these community involvements and technical expertise. We believe that sharing these personal activities will provide a deeper understanding of our technical background and commitment to open ecosystems.

Thank you for your continued support of ILOHA OpenLab.
